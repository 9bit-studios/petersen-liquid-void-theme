## 📦 Orders & Shipping

### Order Tracking

**Track Your Order:**
1. Check your email for shipping confirmation
2. Use tracking number with carrier (UPS, FedEx, USPS)
3. Login to your account for order status
4. Contact support if no tracking after 3 business days

**Order Processing Times:**
- **In-Stock Items:** 1-3 business days
- **Pre-Orders:** Ships according to release schedule
- **Custom Orders:** 2-4 weeks
- **International:** Additional 1-2 days processing

### Order Changes

**Before Shipping:**
- Contact support immediately for address changes
- Product changes may incur restocking fees
- Cancellations accepted until processing begins

**After Shipping:**
- Contact carrier for delivery changes
- We cannot modify orders once shipped
- Return/exchange process applies for changes

## 🔄 Returns & Exchanges

### Return Policy

**30-Day Return Window:**
- Items must be in original, unopened condition
- Original packaging preferred but not required
- Proof of purchase required
- Customer pays return shipping unless item is defective

**Non-Returnable Items:**
- Digital products (STL files, PDFs)
- Custom or personalized items
- Items damaged by customer use
- Products opened and used (unless defective)

### Return Process

1. **Contact Support:** Email with order number and reason
2. **Get Authorization:** Wait for return authorization number
3. **Package Securely:** Include authorization number
4. **Ship Return:** Use trackable shipping method
5. **Refund Processing:** 5-10 business days after receipt

### Defective Items

**Manufacturing Defects:**
- Contact support with photos
- We cover return shipping for defective items
- Replacement or full refund provided
- No time limit for genuine defects
