# Privacy Policy - Petersen Games

**Effective Date:** August 1, 2025

**Last Updated:**  August 10.2025

## 1. Introduction

Petersen Games (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website (petersengames.com), use our mobile applications, or purchase our products.

## 2. Information We Collect

### Personal Information

We collect information that identifies you personally, including:

- **Contact Information:** Name, email address, phone number, mailing address
- **Account Information:** Username, password, account preferences
- **Payment Information:** Billing address, payment method details (processed securely)
- **Order Information:** Purchase history, shipping preferences, product reviews

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Device Information:** IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers
- **Usage Data:** Pages visited, time spent on site, click patterns, referral sources
- **Location Data:** General geographic location based on IP address
- **Cookies and Tracking:** See our Cookie Policy section below

### User-Generated Content

- Product reviews and ratings
- Photos and videos of products
- Forum posts and community contributions
- Customer service communications

## 3. How We Collect Information

### Direct Collection

- When you create an account
- During the checkout process
- When you subscribe to newsletters
- Through customer service interactions
- Via surveys and feedback forms

### Automatic Collection

- Website and app analytics
- Cookies and similar technologies
- Third-party integrations (social media, payment processors)
- Email engagement tracking

### Third-Party Sources

- Social media platforms (when you connect accounts)
- Marketing partners and affiliates
- Fraud prevention services
- Address validation services

## 4. How We Use Your Information

### Order Processing and Fulfillment

- Process and ship your orders
- Send order confirmations and shipping notifications
- Handle returns, exchanges, and customer service inquiries
- Manage pre-orders and crowdfunding deliveries

### Account Management

- Create and maintain your account
- Authenticate your identity
- Provide customer support
- Process newsletter subscriptions

### Marketing and Communications

- Send promotional emails and newsletters (with consent)
- Personalize product recommendations
- Conduct market research and surveys
- Announce new products and special offers

### Website and Service Improvement

- Analyze website usage and performance
- Improve user experience and functionality
- Develop new features and services
- Prevent fraud and ensure security

### Legal and Compliance

- Comply with legal obligations
- Protect our rights and property
- Enforce our terms of service
- Respond to legal requests

## 5. Information Sharing and Disclosure

### Service Providers

We share information with trusted third-party service providers:

- **Shopify:** E-commerce platform and payment processing
- **Shipping Carriers:** UPS, FedEx, USPS, international carriers
- **Email Services:** Newsletter and transactional email delivery
- **Analytics:** Google Analytics, marketing analytics platforms
- **Customer Support:** Help desk and communication tools

### Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of the transaction. You will be notified of any such change.

### Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law:
- To comply with subpoenas, court orders, or legal processes
- To protect our rights, property, or safety
- To investigate fraud or security breaches
- To enforce our terms of service

### With Your Consent

We may share information for other purposes with your explicit consent.

## 6. Data Security

### Security Measures

- SSL encryption for data transmission
- Secure payment processing through certified providers
- Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
- Access controls and employee training
- Data backup and disaster recovery procedures

### Payment Security

- We do not store complete credit card information
- Payment data is processed by PCI-compliant providers
- Tokenization and encryption protect sensitive data
- Fraud monitoring and prevention systems

### Data Retention

- Personal information is retained as long as necessary for business purposes
- Account information is kept while your account is active
- Order history is maintained for customer service and legal requirements
- Inactive accounts may be deleted after extended periods

## 7. Your Privacy Rights

### Access and Correction

- View and update your account information
- Request copies of personal information we hold
- Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
- Download your data in portable formats

### Marketing Controls

- Unsubscribe from promotional emails
- Opt out of targeted advertising
- Manage communication preferences
- Update newsletter subscriptions

### Account Management

- Delete your account and associated data
- Request information about data processing
- Object to certain types of data processing
- Withdraw consent where applicable

### Regional Rights

Depending on your location, you may have additional rights under:
- **GDPR (European Union):** Right to erasure, data portability, restriction of processing
- **CCPA (California):** Right to know, delete, and opt-out of sale
- **Other Local Laws:** Additional rights as provided by applicable legislation

## 8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

### Types of Cookies

- **Essential Cookies:** Required for website functionality
- **Analytics Cookies:** Help us understand website usage
- **Marketing Cookies:** Enable personalized advertising
- **Preference Cookies:** Remember your settings and preferences

### Cookie Management

- Most browsers allow you to control cookies
- You can delete existing cookies and prevent future ones
- Some website features may not work without cookies
- Mobile app settings control similar tracking technologies

### Third-Party Tracking

- Google Analytics for website analytics
- Social media platforms for sharing and authentication
- Advertising networks for targeted marketing
- Email service providers for engagement tracking

## 9. International Data Transfers

### Cross-Border Processing

- Your information may be processed in countries other than your own
- We ensure adequate protection through appropriate safeguards
- Standard contractual clauses or adequacy decisions provide protection
- Data is processed according to this Privacy Policy regardless of location

### Safeguards for International Transfers

- Contractual obligations with service providers
- Compliance with applicable data protection laws
- Regular monitoring of international processing
- Prompt notification of any data breaches

## 10. Children’s Privacy

### Age Restrictions

- Our services are not directed to children under 13
- We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13
- If we discover we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly
- Parents should supervise their children’s online activities

### Parental Controls

- Parents may review and request deletion of their child’s information
- Account creation requires age verification
- Educational content may be available for appropriate age groups
- Customer service can assist with family account management

- We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices
- Significant changes will be prominently posted on our website
- Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of changes
- Previous versions will be archived and available upon request

- Opportunity to review changes before they take effect
