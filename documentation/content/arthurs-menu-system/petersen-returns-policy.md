# Returns & Exchanges - Petersen Games

We want you to love your cosmic horror gaming experience. If you’re not completely satisfied, we’re here to help.

---

## 🔄 Return Policy Overview

### 30-Day Return Window

- **Timeframe:** 30 days from delivery date
- **Condition:** Items must be in original, unopened condition
- **Packaging:** Original packaging strongly preferred
- **Receipt:** Proof of purchase required (order number or receipt)

### Easy Return Process

1. **Contact Us:** Email support@petersengames.com with order details
2. **Get Authorization:** We’ll provide a return authorization number
3. **Pack Securely:** Include authorization number in package
4. **Ship Back:** Use a trackable shipping method
5. **Receive Refund:** 5-10 business days after we receive your return

---

## ✅ What Can Be Returned

### Eligible for Return

- **Unopened Games:** In original shrink wrap or sealed condition
- **Unused Miniatures:** In original packaging, undamaged
- **Defective Items:** Any manufacturing defects, no time limit
- **Wrong Items:** Items we shipped incorrectly
- **Damaged in Shipping:** Items damaged during transit

### Return Conditions

- **Original Condition:** Items must be resellable
- **Complete Sets:** All components included (rules, pieces, etc.)
- **No Wear:** No signs of use, handling, or table wear
- **Clean:** Free from dust, pet hair, smoke odors

---

## ❌ What Cannot Be Returned

### Non-Returnable Items

- **Digital Products:** STL files, PDFs, digital downloads
- **Opened Games:** Once shrink wrap is removed and game is used
- **Custom Items:** Personalized or made-to-order products
- **Final Sale Items:** Clearance items marked as final sale
- **Gift Cards:** Non-refundable unless required by law

### Special Circumstances

- **Pre-painted Miniatures:** Cannot be returned if removed from packaging
- **Convention Exclusives:** Limited edition items (case-by-case basis)
- **Used STL Files:** Downloaded digital content cannot be returned

---

## 🔧 Defective Items

### Manufacturing Defects

**We Cover All Defect Returns:**
- Warped or damaged game boards
- Missing components or miniatures
- Printing errors in rulebooks
- Broken miniatures due to manufacturing issues
- Miscut cards or tokens

### Defect Return Process

1. **Document the Issue:** Take clear photos of the defect
2. **Contact Support:** Email with photos and order number
3. **Rapid Response:** We typically respond within 24 hours
4. **Free Replacement:** We cover return shipping for defects
5. **Keep Original:** Often you can keep the defective item

### No Time Limit on Defects

- Manufacturing defects covered indefinitely
- Proof of purchase from Petersen Games required
- Defects from normal wear and tear not covered
- Customer damage not covered under defect policy

---

## 💰 Refunds & Processing

### Refund Timeline

- **Processing Time:** 5-10 business days after receiving return
- **Payment Method:** Refund to original payment method
- **Bank Processing:** Additional 3-5 days for bank processing
- **PayPal Refunds:** Usually immediate once processed

### Refund Amounts

**Full Refunds:**
- Purchase price minus any shipping fees paid
- Tax refunded if applicable
- Promotional discounts maintained in calculation

**Partial Refunds:**
- Missing components: Partial refund for incomplete returns
- Restocking fees: 15% for opened collectible items
- Return shipping deducted for customer error returns

### Refund Exceptions

- **Gift Purchases:** Refunded to original purchaser’s payment method
- **Store Credit Option:** Available for faster processing
- **International Orders:** May require wire transfer for large amounts

---

## 🔄 Exchanges

### Direct Exchanges

**When Available:**
- Size or variant changes for same product
- Color preferences for available options
- Upgrade to deluxe editions (pay difference)

### Exchange Process

1. **Contact Support:** Explain desired exchange
2. **Check Availability:** We’ll confirm stock of desired item
3. **Ship Both Ways:** Return original, we ship replacement
4. **Price Differences:** Pay or receive refund for price gaps

### Exchange Limitations

- **Same Product Line:** Exchanges within same game series
- **Stock Dependent:** Limited to available inventory
- **Pre-Orders:** Cannot exchange pre-orders for in-stock items

---

## 🎁 Gift Returns

### Gift Return Policy

- **Gift Recipients:** Can return gifts without original payment info
- **Store Credit:** Gift returns typically receive store credit
- **Gift Receipt:** Include gift receipt for easier processing
- **Original Price:** Returns processed at original purchase price

### Gift Return Process

1. **Contact Support:** Mention this is a gift return
2. **Proof of Gift:** Gift receipt or order confirmation helpful
3. **Store Credit Issued:** Usually fastest option for gift returns
4. **No Questions Asked:** We don’t ask gift recipients for reasons

---

## 🌍 International Returns

### International Return Shipping

- **Customer Responsibility:** International customers pay return shipping
- **Customs Forms:** Mark as “Return to Sender”
- **Insurance Recommended:** Insure valuable returns
- **Tracking Required:** Must use trackable shipping method

### International Refund Considerations

- **Currency Conversion:** Refunded in original purchase currency
- **Bank Fees:** Customer responsible for currency conversion fees
- **Customs Duties:** Cannot refund duties paid on original shipment
- **Processing Time:** May take additional time due to customs

---

## ⚠️ Return Restrictions

### Condition Requirements

**Items Must Be:**
- In original, sellable condition
- Free from damage, wear, or modification
- Complete with all original components
- Clean and odor-free

**Automatic Rejection:**
- Items showing signs of use or play
- Incomplete sets missing components
- Damaged by customer (not shipping)
- Items purchased over 30 days ago (except defects)

### Restocking Fees

**When Applied:**
- Opened collectible items: 15% restocking fee
- Custom orders cancelled after production: 25% fee
- Large orders requiring reprocessing: 10% fee
- Multiple returns from same customer: Discretionary fee
