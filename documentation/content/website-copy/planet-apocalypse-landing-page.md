The invasion follows Hell's strict organizational structure:

Hell's invasion of Earth represents the ultimate sophistication of evil. Rather than crude destruction, the demonic forces seek to demonstrate their superiority through the artistry of conquest. Each battle becomes a lesson in aesthetic warfare, where humanity must appreciate the terrible beauty of their opponents even as they fight for survival.

The true horror isn't just humanity's potential extinction—it's the realization that Hell's cultural sophistication far surpasses mortal civilization, making resistance feel not just hopeless but almost crude in comparison.
