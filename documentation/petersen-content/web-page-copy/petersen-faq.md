## 🎮 About Our Games

### Who is Sandy Petersen?
Sandy Petersen is a legendary game designer with over 40 years of experience. He created the Call of Cthulhu RPG in 1981, was lead designer on classic video games DOOM and Quake, and contributed to Age of Empires II. His board games are known for innovative asymmetric gameplay and premium production quality.

### What makes Petersen Games different?
- **Asymmetric Gameplay:** Each faction plays completely differently
- **Premium Miniatures:** 28mm to 150mm scale with exceptional detail
- **Cosmic Horror Theme:** Authentic Lovecraftian atmosphere
- **Strategic Depth:** Games reward multiple strategies and playstyles
- **Quality Components:** Museum-quality miniatures and materials

### Are your games suitable for beginners?
Most of our games are designed for intermediate to advanced players, but we offer learning resources:
- **Starter Recommendations:** Planet Apocalypse or Hyperspace
- **Tutorial Videos:** Available on our YouTube channel
- **Complexity Ratings:** Listed on each product page
- **Learning Support:** Active community helps new players

## 🎯 Products & Games

### What's the difference between Core Games and Expansions?
- **Core Games:** Complete, standalone gaming experiences with everything needed to play
- **Expansions:** Additional content requiring the base game, adding new factions, maps, or mechanics
- **Deluxe Editions:** Enhanced versions with premium components and exclusive content

### Are the miniatures pre-painted?
- **Standard Products:** Miniatures come unpainted but detailed and ready for play
- **Premium Lines:** Some special editions include pre-painted miniatures
- **Painting Optional:** Games are fully playable without painting
- **Painting Support:** We offer painting guides and community resources

### What scale are your miniatures?
- **Standard Units:** 28mm scale (compatible with other gaming miniatures)
- **Heroes/Leaders:** Often 32mm for prominence
- **Great Old Ones:** 75mm to 150mm for dramatic presence
- **Scale Consistency:** All miniatures in each game line use consistent scaling

### Can I buy individual miniatures?
- **Replacement Parts:** Individual miniatures available for damaged or lost pieces
- **Faction Packs:** Some factions sold separately as expansions
- **Limited Singles:** Certain special miniatures available individually
- **Custom Orders:** Contact support for specific miniature needs

## 💻 Digital Products

### What are STL files?
STL files are 3D printable models that you can print on your own 3D printer. Our STL files include pre-supported versions optimized for resin printing.

### What do I need to print STL files?
- **3D Printer:** Resin printer recommended for detail
- **Slicing Software:** Free software to prepare files for printing
- **Resin/Filament:** Materials for printing
- **Basic Skills:** Some 3D printing experience helpful

### Can I sell printed miniatures?
No, our STL files are licensed for personal use only. Commercial use is not permitted under our license agreement.

### How long can I download STL files?
- **Initial Download:** 30 days from purchase
- **Account Access:** Files remain in your account indefinitely
- **Re-download:** Contact support if you need files re-sent
- **Backup Recommended:** Save files to your own storage

## 🌍 International Customers

### Do you charge VAT/GST for international orders?
- **VAT:** Not collected for EU customers (paid at customs)
- **GST:** Not collected for Canadian customers (paid at customs)
- **Duties:** Customer responsible for all import duties and taxes
- **DDP Option:** Duties and taxes included shipping available for select countries

### What about customs delays?
- **Normal Processing:** 1-5 days in most countries
- **Holiday Delays:** Extended delays during holiday periods
- **Documentation:** All packages include proper customs declarations
- **Tracking:** International tracking provided for all shipments

### Can I return international orders?
Yes, but international customers are responsible for return shipping costs. We recommend insured, trackable shipping for returns.
